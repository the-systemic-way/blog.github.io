The Beginning

26 October 2016 on Beginnings

This blog is my holding space that allows me to share, express, reflect and connect deeper within and with you. It is an exercise in contemplation, reflection and a mindful relaxation of learnings and experiences that evolve as I deepen my relationship with the systemic work and thinking deeply inspired by the work of Systemic Constellations or Family Constellations by Bert Hellinger in Germany and my trainer,master in India.

How it all began

In January 2014 I was going through a tough time interpersonally at cross roads again between myself and my family and I was seeking strength to face the unknown, a virtue that seemed disappearing from within gripped with anxiety and a haziness that was so distinguishable and yet alien. It was emotional turmoil gripped with a deep fear and a strong sense of disconnect with myself in my body as well as my relationship with my family.

On a fine morning I walked blindly into a Family Constellation Workshop by Annie Cariappa and sooner that I thought I was in the client's chair pouring my heart with words that kept betraying my lips.
I walked into the unknown with a trust and uncertainty that I had never felt before. In the coming months what unfolded in that constellation was determined to stay with me forever and I continued on the journey of systemic living, experiencing, expressing, learning and sharing.

Where I am now

I am at a junction where I seek the blessings and wisdom of my ancestors from whom I come with all my values, strength, virtues, flaws and all of my inheritance. To whom I belong and from whom I seek the wisdom of living and learning. I bow to all my many gurus, masters that touched my lives and shaped in one way or another. I see how my life experiences enriched and exposed those wounds of my soul I am grateful to these life experiences. I carry within me the various learnings, skills that I gained, learned, picked along this journey of life, I traversed many paths and donned many roles and all that it makes me who I am today. Soaked in this wealth I step ahead and look at my future in my full potential as a women as I am exactly as I am.

The journey ahead

The culmination of these experiences call me towards a exploring and engaging with the complexities and depths of interpersonal relationships, interdependent living, co-creation with an inbuilt harmony of the natural world.

My personal work journeys

  • Theory and practice of Family Constellation offered by Spanda, India and Ochre, Ireland. Systemic Theory and Practice in Family Constellation work, Bangalore, India
  • Heal your Inner Child - A 3 day residential, experiential healing work.
  • TA 101 Mindful Living - A 2 day workshop in Transactional Analysis.
  • A personal work journey through Art, The 21 week shaktiship Awakening the Shakti Woman
